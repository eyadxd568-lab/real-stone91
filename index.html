Real Stone Marble & Granite

Real Stone Marble & Granite LLC specialized in the supply and installation of natural stones for exterior and interior stone cladding, flooring, ornamental and specialized architectural works. We have a team of professionals with extensive knowledge and experience in Marble & Granite Industry and are actively engaged in imports of Marbles, supply, fabrication and installation in the Middle East region.

We undertake all Floorings, Kitchen Tops, Vanity Tops, Counter Tops, Wall Cladding, Crazy Paving, Tread & Risers, Cladding and Decorative Marble requirements. Our finished products whether external or internal demonstrates our professionalism, reliability and level of services we provide to our clients. We create and install Marbles to the client's exact specification within the specified time and allocated budget. We take pride in successful completion of projects such as commercial buildings, residential towers and villas, mosques, palaces etc in U.A.E. We aim at serving our clients with all our abilities, to ensure their complete satisfaction.

Real Stone Marble and Granite has the vision to become the largest supplier of natural marble from our quarries in Italy , Europe and Vietnam to the Middle East by providing expertise and consulting that is very high level for the Public sector and the private sector to provide high quality products and service to the highest standards

Real Stone Marble and Granite established in the UAE as a leading company in the field .Real Stone works for marble with leading companies in the local market, external market and large contracting companies that have gained us sharing experiences, development, progress and growth.
